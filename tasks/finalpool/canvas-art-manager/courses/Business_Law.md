# Business Law

Credits: 3

Learning Outcomes:
- Identify legal issues in common business scenarios.
- Draft basic commercial contracts.

| 7    | Mid-Term                        |
| 8    | Agency                          |
| 9    | Employment Law                  |
| 10   | Business Organizations          |
| 11   | Intellectual Property           |
| 12   | Securities                      |
| 13   | Negotiation Lab                 |

Policies: Open-book final; IRAC format required.
